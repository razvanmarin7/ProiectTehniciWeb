Montreal Botanical Garden

The Montreal Botanical Garden is one of the city’s most peaceful and inspiring places. It brings together themed gardens, greenhouses and walking paths where visitors can enjoy nature in every season.

From the Japanese and Chinese gardens to the rose garden and large arboretum, the Botanical Garden is a perfect place to relax, take photos and discover plants from all around the world.

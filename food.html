Taste of Montreal

Poutine

A classic Quebec dish made of fries, cheese curds and hot gravy — simple, comforting and iconic.

Montreal Bagels

Smaller and sweeter than New York bagels, boiled in honey water and baked in wood-fired ovens for a special flavor.

Smoked Meat Sandwich

A delicious Montreal specialty prepared with slow-smoked beef, mustard and pickles, served in many famous delis across the city.
